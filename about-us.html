Who are we ?

We are a voluntary-run small community with achievement on innovation that can help with environmental sustainability. Through the last decade, we have tried to deliver transformation changes to workplaces, education institutes and educate the public about harms that can have a negative impact on our planet Earth. Established in 2007, we were founded through an investment from the Scottish government and a charity-run environmental organization.

Our primary focus is to reach net-zero climate impact and educate the whole public about a small daily task necessary to reduce waste disposal in the oceans. We are trying to bring together academic researchers, talented students, and external partners from industry or government to collaborate in many sectors where it is possible to innovate or educate people for delivering more sustainable growth.
